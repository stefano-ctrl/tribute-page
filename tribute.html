Winston Churchill

The man who saved the free world

Chronology

  • 1874

    Winston Churchill is born in Oxfordshire

  • 1894

    Churchill graduates from the Royal Military College in Sandhurst

  • 1895

    Churchill's father dies

  • 1899

    Churchill campaigns for Parliament

  • 1900

    Churchill wins a seat in the British House of Commons

  • 1908

    Churchill marries Clementine Hozier

  • 1940

    Churchill begins first term as prime minister

  • 1941

    Churchill stays at the White House begins

  • 1945

    Churchill attends the Yalta Conference

  • 1945

    Churchill loses the United Kingdom general election

  • 1946

    Churchill gives his famous "iron curtain" speech

  • 1951

    Churchill is elected prime minister again

  • 1953

    Churchill wins the Nobel Prize for literature

  • 1955

    Churchill retires as prime minister

  • 1965

    Winston Churchill dies
